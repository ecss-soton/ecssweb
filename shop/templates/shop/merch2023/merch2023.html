Slogans

We love slogans, and you can have optional slogans on the back of our hoodies, jumpers and quarter zips.
Here is a list of slogans available this year created and picked by our members:
  • I Have No Idea How To Fix Your Laptop
  • If Its Not Broken, There Is Still Time To Change That.
  • Byte Me
  • A Monad Is A Monoid In The Category of Endofuctors
  • Can I Get Your Port Number?
  • And more!
Don't fancy having slogans? Leaving it blank is an option, or you can have "Electronics and Computer Science" on the back!
